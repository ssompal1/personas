MICROWAVES

An inconspicuous interface we rarely think about.

I conducted natural observation and user interviews to better understand how users interact with a microwave interface. This research was done in the Brown Ethnic Studies Department kitchen, located by Brown's Main Green.

The Interface

I began by modeling the microwave interface which enables users to adjust time and intensity settings. Users interact through button presses to control these functions.

User Interviews

I conducted interviews with 3 microwave users, asking 10 questions and analyzing the recurring themes in their responses.

Frozen food, leftovers, liquids, heating pads.
